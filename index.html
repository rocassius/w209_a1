Juggling Progress Trajectory


Description

I recorded how many times I could continuously toss juggling 3 balls up and clap before catching them while balancing on one leg.

Insights

  1. The longest clapping streaks occur during the latter attempt because I'd already had during latter attempts.
  2. My right leg average is higher than my left average in the first half, but my left average overtakes my right average in latter attempts.
  3. Although my left leg may appear less reliable because I achieved 0 claps twice while on my left leg, I also achieved my personal record of 16 when balancing on my left.

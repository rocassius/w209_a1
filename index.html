Juggling Progress Trajectory

Description

I recorded how many times I could successfully toss juggling 3 balls up and clap before catching them in a single continuous streak while balancing on one leg.

Insights

  1. The longest clapping streaks occur during high attempt numbers because I'd had substantial practice by then.
  2. My right leg average is higher then my left average in the first half, but my left average overtake my right in the later attempts.
  3. Although my left leg may appear less reliable because I achieved 0 claps twice while on my left leg, I also achieved my personal record of 16 while on my left.

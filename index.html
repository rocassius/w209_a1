Juggling Improvement Trajectory

I decided to collect data about my juggling. Specifically, I recorded how many times I could toss all 3 balls in the air and clap before catching them and repeating this continuously while balancing on one leg. The data shows that the number of claps I could do was correlated with how many times I had attempted this. My best runs are clustered at toward the end of my experiment when I had already had substantial practice. Additionally, the data suggest that balancing on my right leg was no more or less helpful than balancing on my left.
